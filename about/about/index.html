About Morja

Zala Hrastar

I am a 22-year-old fashion graduate, creating under artistic name MORJA. I come from a small village in southeastern Slovenia, finding inspiration in my family’s past for a better and greener tomorrow. In my designs, I use textile waste, deadstock materials and second hand clothes, to create one-of-a-kind colourful, playful, handsewn garments that shine positive energy and youthfulness.

In 2020 I graduated from Textile & Fashion Design at the Faculty of Natural Sciences and Engineering, University of Ljubljana.

My other interests include photography, illustration, modern poetry and music. I am a violinist and singer in a Slovenian band Eni od Sedmih Milijard. In the past I participated in several fashion shows including Ljubljana Fashion Week, STYLO 2019, ŠKUC x NTF summer selling exhibition and fashion show…
