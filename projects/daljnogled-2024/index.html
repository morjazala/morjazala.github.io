A GARDEN OF FABRICS / VRTIČKANJE, PRTIČKANJE: DALJNOGLED award

Photo: Kristine Lazzarini

In 2023, I received the DALJNOGLED 2023 award for my master’s thesis “A Garden of Fabrics/Vrtičkanje, Prtičkanje,” an honor given to young designers by the Designers Society of Slovenia.

On Thursday, January 25th, 2024, at 5 PM, the opening of my work was held on a display panel in Cankar Centre, where the exhibition remained on view until mid-March.
