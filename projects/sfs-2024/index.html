A Walk Down Memory Lane

“For it doesn’t hurt to dream when the moon watches and glows. So I am dreaming of brighter days.” — This City of Dreams from the EP “A Walk Down Memory Lane”.

The upcycled collection “A Walk Down Memory Lane” was showcased at the Slow Fashion Show in A House, Stockholm, on March 7th, 2024.

I am deeply grateful for the opportunity to be part of this event, alongside fellow creatives who are working toward a brighter future in fashion.

Special thanks to Ela Mejač for being the best assistant one could ask for.

This project was completed during my first year of master’s studies at the Faculty of Natural Sciences and Engineering, under the mentorship of Prof. Marija Jenko.
