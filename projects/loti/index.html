LOTI

Model: Loti Gorenc
Photo: Zala Hrastar
Makeup: Sara Živič
Styling: Zala Hrastar

Besides making one-of-a-kind out of second-hand and waste-textile garments, I love photography. And in the past year, when free of any university work, I decided to spare some time experimenting in the studio and outdoors. I am particullary interested in beauty-shot editorials; capturing peoples' energy, charisma and communicating it through camera.

For this photo session I collaborated with very talented, young make-up artist Sara Živič. She introduced me Loti, her lovely friend. I styled her in some of my designs and Sara completed the look with matching face art.

Working with them was a blessing and I really love the final result. We were in the studio for so long, the security guard almost locked us in the building. That’s what it means to have fun; forgetting the time and just being in the moment.
