Second hand shirts getting a new chance, a new life for a better and greener tomorrow.

I started the project in the beginning of 2019 realising my closet has way too many bright coloured, karo patterned second hand shirts. So I got an idea; why not redesign them, upcycle them, patchwork them with some other waste cut-off materials that I collect with whatever I do? So I choose 4 shirts and transformed them into new ones.

And here I am, one year later; still in love with redesigning, upcycling. And the love just keeps growing big.

*If your closet has some long-forgotten shirts that need some retouch; I am glad to help!
