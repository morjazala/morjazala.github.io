FLORA ADA

Model: Karin Hočever
Photo: Zala Hrastar

Flora Ada is a figure embraced by flowers, like a goddess of an (un)ordinary day.

The clothing collection represents a harmonious mix of past motifs set in the present days. Each piece is a delicate blend of vintage patterns and contemporary cuts, weaving together stories of yesteryears with the vibrancy of modern life. The collection features dresses with hems inspired by old garden paintings paired with minimalist bodices, creating a seamless dialogue between the old and new.

“Flora Ada" is more than just fashion; it’s a narrative, capturing the essence of time and beauty in every stitch.

Thanks to my dear stylist and assistant Ela Mejač.
