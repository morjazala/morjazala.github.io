MORJA is a brand for everyone who loves waking up with the rising sun, who dances with sounds of the wind and sings with the bright moon.

MORJA is for everyone who is not affraid to show their patchwork of emotion and who finds gratitude even in the darkest of the days. It's for everyone who loves layering clothes like an onion does and is not affraid to stand out while blending in.

Zala, creator of MORJA am 23 years young, inspired by approach to life and living of my parents, I believe in positive changes through small, individual steps towards producing less waste; with appreciating what we already have by reusing and remaking items to last longer.

Thank you for being with me on this journey called LIVING.

I am open for collaborations, so don't hesitate to contact me. ✿

For any questions, (custmo made) orders or interests in collaboration. I’ll be happy to hear from you.

Do you have any preloved clothes you don't wear anymore (they are too small/damaged) but would love to give them another chance? I'll be happy to give them a second life and save them from the landfill. Write to me, and we will figure it out.

I do custom made orders too - check in with me for more info.
