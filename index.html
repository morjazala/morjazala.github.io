MORJA is a slow upcycling brand of pre-loved clothing and textiles that shares happiness and warmth with great respect for materials, the environment and people.

MORJA items are unique patchworks of everyday emotions in a pattern mix-match, handmade for you who are not afraid to show your true colours.

Zala, the creator of MORJA, is 26 years young and believes in positive change through small, individual steps towards producing less waste, appreciating what we already have by reusing and remaking items to last longer.

Thank you for joining this journey called LIVING.

I am open to collaboration, so don't hesitate to contact me. ✿

If you have any questions or are interested in working together, I look forward to hearing from you.
